Eid Mubarak

Making كحك during Eid Mubarak is one of the most cherished traditions in many homes, filling the atmosphere with warmth, joy, and the delicious scent of freshly baked treats. Families often gather together in the kitchen, sharing laughter and stories while shaping the dough into beautiful patterns and filling it with dates or nuts. The process itself becomes a celebration, as everyone takes part young and old passing down customs from one generation to the next. When the كحك is finally baked and dusted with powdered sugar, it symbolizes not just a sweet dessert, but the happiness, togetherness, and blessings that Eid Mubarak brings *this paragraph by Ai*
